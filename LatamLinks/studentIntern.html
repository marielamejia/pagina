Position overview:

- As a Student Intern at LatAmLinks, you will be involved in a dynamic environment focused on simplifying business development for companies expanding into Mexico and Latin America.
- This role provides an excellent opportunity to gain hands-on experience in various aspects of international business, from market analysis to client relationship management.
- You'll be part of a team dedicated to providing strategic support and solutions to businesses venturing into these vibrant markets.


Key responsibilities:

- Assist in market research and analysis to identify business opportunities.
- Support the development and implementation of business strategies.
- Help in coordinating and managing communication with clients and partners.
- Participate in the planning and execution of business development initiatives.
- Assist in the creation of presentation materials and reports.
- Engage in administrative tasks to support the team as needed.


Requirements:

- A keen interest in business development and international markets.
- Basic understanding of business principles; prior experience is a plus, but not necessary.
- Analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.


Learning opportunities:

- Gain real-world experience in international business development.
- Develop a deep understanding of the business landscape in Latin America and Mexico.
- Network with professionals and experts in the field.
- Enhance communication, research, and analytical skills in a professional setting.
